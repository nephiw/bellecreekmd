Meetings

The Covenant Enforcement Committee (CEC) meets once a month on the third wednesday. These meetings are open to the public and we encourage the home owners join us if they want to hear what is going on or express some other concern. This committee is responsible for covenant enforcement and handling difficult design/improvement requests. This is what most people would call our HOA.

Due to the government recommendations around COVID-19, the March 18th CEC meeting will be cancelled. If you have questions or concerns regarding covenant enforcement, please contact the CEC at cec@bellecreek.org.

The next meeting is on the third Wednesday of the month at 6:00 pm at the Family Center

For questions regarding covenants and design/improvement requests, email cec@bellecreek.org or Laurie Tatlock at laurie@mulhernmre.com.
