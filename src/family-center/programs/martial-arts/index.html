Martial Arts

Martial arts classes teaches more than just how to defend yourself, it also builds confidence, teaches control, and builds community. There are classes available in Karate, Tae Kwon Do, Aikido, Ground Control and Self-Defense. There are both male and females instructors who are certified black belts by the American Sport Karate Association. They can help guide students of all ages. Individual and family rates are available.
