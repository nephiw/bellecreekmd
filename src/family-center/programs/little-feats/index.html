Little Feats

At the Belle Creek Family Center, we are committed to providing a healthy and safe environment for your child. In Little Feats Playgroup, children will explore colors, shapes, numbers and letters through a blend of theme based curriculum and will be encouraged to explore and focus on the importance of community and self-expression. This program helps develop self-esteem, communication, fine/gross motor skills and group interaction.

We also offer a bring-your-own lunch program we call Little Feats Eats where children will socialize while they eat food together. When they are not eating, they will have more time to play with each other, building friendships and learning to interact while eating.

We offer two seperate programs for potty-trained children ages 3-5:

  • Little Feats Playgroup (9 am - 11:30 am)
  • Little Feats Eats (11:30 am - 1 pm)
