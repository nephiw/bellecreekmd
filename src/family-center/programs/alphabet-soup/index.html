Alphabet Soup

At the Belle Creek Family Center, we are committed to providing a healthy and safe environment for your child. In Alphabet Soup, children will explore colors, shapes, numbers and letters through a blend of theme based curriculum and will be encouraged to explore and focus on the importance of community and self-expression. This program helps develop self-esteem, communication, fine/gross motor skills and group interaction.

We offer three seperate programs for children ages 3-5:

  • Early Birds (8 am - 9 am)
  • Alphabet Soup (9 am - 11:30 am)
  • Lunch Bunch (11:30 am - 1 pm)
