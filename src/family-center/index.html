Family Center

Our residents’ and employee’s health and safety is highest concern of the Belle Creek Metropolitan District. Due to the recent developments of COVID-19 (coronavirus), our Governor declaring a state of emergency, along with the closure of Commerce City public facilities, and the Belle Creek Charter Schools closure, effective immediately, the Belle Creek Family Center is CLOSED.

Hours of Operation

  • Monday - Thursday from 6 am to 9 pm
  • Friday from 6 am to 7 pm
  • Saturday and Sunday from 9 am to 5 pm

Street Address

10693 Belle Creek Blvd., Henderson, CO 80640

Phone Number

Childrens Programs

  • Little Feats Playgroup - this program gives your child an opportunity to play and learn with other children.
  • Bizybees - this is a full day daycare for preschool student.
  • YMCA Before and After School Child Care - this program is offered by the YMCA and run out of the Belle Creek Family Center.

Adult Programs

  • Martial Arts
  • Men's Pickup Basketball
  • Pickleball

If you are currently enrolled in a program and do not see it here, then it is likely still ongoing and operating as normal. We are in the middle of a transition and are updating this site as we learn more. If you do not see your program please contact either Kyle Burnett (manager@bellecreek.org) or Laurie Tatlock (303-649-9857, laurie@mulhernmre.com). We will try to contact the operator of the program and get the details posted here.

If you currently offer classes or rent rooms at the Family Center, or want to start, please contact Laurie Tatlock (303-649-9857, laurie@mulhernmre.com) to talk about how to continue to offer your classes or continue your rentals.

Rentals

The Family Center has several rooms, including the gym, that can be rented. We also can rent our jump castle and other event related equipment. Please stop by the Family Center for more details.
