Family Center

The family center regular hours are:

  • Monday - Thursday from 6 am to 9 pm
  • Friday from 6 am to 7 pm
  • Saturday and Sunday from 9 am to 5 pm

The gym and meeting rooms may be rented outside of these hours given advanced notice.

The Family Center is located at the corner of Belle Creek Blvd and 107th Ave, our address is:
10693 Belle Creek Blvd., Henderson, CO 80640

The front desk can be reach by phone at 303-289-3584.

Programs

The community program schedule is coming soon. Childrens Programs like Alphabet Soup and the Before and After Care are currently our priority. Updates will be announced as we learn more.

If you currently offer classes or rent rooms at the family center, please contact Laurie Tatlock (303-649-9857, laurie@mulhernmre.com) to talk about how to continue to offer your classes or continue your rentals.

Rentals

The Family Center has several rooms, including the gym, that can be rented. We also can rent our jump castle and other event related equipment. Please stop by the family center for more details.
